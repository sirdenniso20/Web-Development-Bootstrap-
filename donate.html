Donate to Winter Savers

Your donation saves Life.

Winter Savers provides free clothing to homeless and lower-income people in our community. When you work with Second Chances, or contribute your time or money, you help our neighbors take a giant step forward from where their lives are now toward a brighter future.

Financial contributions

When you contribute to Winter Savers, you make it possible to provide clothing to neighbors who need our help. For a donation of $50, you supply one complete outfit. For $150, you send three outfits to a person or family in need. Winter Savers is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Financial donations are 100% tax-deductible
